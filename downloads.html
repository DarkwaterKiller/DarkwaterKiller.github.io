Here you can download most of the data packs that DarkwaterKiller has made in his videos. All buttons labeled as " Download" will immediately download the pack from my GitHub repository. Buttons labeled " Dropbox" will take you to my Dropbox page for that pack.
For more information on each pack, hover your mouse over the "" icon.
Attention: To install a data pack in your world, simply download the data pack and put the downloaded zip file in your world's "datapacks" folder. If you still need help, click here.
Attention: In order to help keep my content free, downloading a pack will take you through AdFly. If you do not want to/don't feel comfortable using AdFly, use the Dropbox link instead.
